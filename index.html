Website Hosting & Maintenance - Having your website on the internet every month costs money. There is also maintenance that needs to be done in order to keep your website the healthiest it can be. We take care of all of that for you here so you don’t have to lift a finger.

SSL Certification - This is a layer of protection for your website. Although not perfect, it tells Google your website is safe to visit. Another monthly cost that we take care of for you!

Website Updates - Keep your customers updated with news regarding your business. This could be new products & services, specials & holiday sales or updates to your pricing.

Website Domain - This will be the link to your website. There is a yearly fee that is covered with any plan you choose with us.

Social Media Integration - We will add your social media profiles on the website however you’d like. We can generate a huge pop up screen or list your social media on the bottom of the page so you can gather the following you deserve.

Backup Page - In case of your website being hacked, we will rebuild your website again from scratch free of charge. However, in some cases you may need to resend content if the website is not accessible.

Landing Page - We will build you a one page website designed to bring in new & current customers to your store. Any additional pages come at $50/page. This is useful for website ranking on Google and also to provide more information to your visitors regarding your business.

Mobile Responsive Design - Our websites are built to be seen on phones and desktop. Website design businesses typically have this as a hidden fee, which is false advertising because a website cannot function without mobile responsiveness. Not us though, we don’t play games.

Google Maps Business Integration - If you don’t have your business on Google, you came to the right place. We will guide you in setting it up so when customers search for a local business related to your niche, yours will pop up, increasing your chances of a sale.

On-Page Search Engine Optimization - SEO is important for your website ranking on Google. This has many factors such as content, time and visitor engagement. We do On-Page SEO for your website.

Weekly Graphics (FB/IG) - We will post on your Facebook and/or Instagram pages promoting your business with weekly graphic designs related to your niche. This includes holiday posts, sales or anything you’d like us to post.

STEP 1- PORTFOLIO: Check out our portfolio and you’ll see our quality in design. Our websites are made to grab the visitors’ attention so they can visit your shop or contact you through your website. Professionally made at a lower price than other website design businesses.

STEP 2- CONTACT: Complete the questionnaire on our website so we can gather more details about your business to determine how the content on your website will look. Alternatively, you can send us a text with a bit about your business. I have a fear of phone calls, however if you insist, we can schedule a call through text.

STEP 3- DEPOSIT & CONTRACT: Yumsah’s will provide a month-month contract agreement. If you agree to it, we will start designing your website; which can take anywhere between 5-14 days. The first month will be paid for upfront & the second month starts 6 weeks after the deposit was made. The quicker the responses, the more free website hosting you receive!

STEP 4- COPYWRITING: At this stage, you will need to start writing ideas for what you want on your website. This includes writing paragraphs about the services you provide or about your business. Yumsah’s can provide some help, however you know about your business and the services you provide the most. Grammar and sentence structure does not matter, you can send whatever information you can and we can rewrite it for you so it looks professional.

STEP 5- REVISIONS: Your website’s first draft is finished! Some sections may have text or image holders. We will provide a temporary link to your website so you can decide what needs to be added, changed or removed. This can be colors, design, sections, buttons, text size/font,etc.

STEP 6- WEBSITE DEPLOYMENT: After multiple revisions, we will arrive at the final and best version of your website. Congratulations, your website is up and ready for business!
